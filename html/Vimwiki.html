Vimwiki Notes

Commands

  • \wd Delete !WikiWord you're in.
  • \wr Rename !WikiWord you're in.

Notes

  • To disable the sound when switching out of Vimperator using <A-Tab>, see :h :VisualBell.
  • To disable a !WikiWord to be a link, add let g:vimwiki_camel_case=0 in the config.
